About Me

2020.
Graduated into a pandemic as a Civil Engineer, the job market was filled with so much fear and uncertainties, but so much expectations back home. With all the lockdowns in place, most companies were struggling to survive. So I thought this might be the best time to explore something I've always wanted to try, something out of my comfort zone - sales.

2021.
Forget about engineering, I decided to take the plunge and became a real estate negotiator. It was challenging, espescially with the economy at an all time low and unsupportive parents certainly didn't help either. My decision was probably questionable to some, but as time goes by, I became more resilient and managed to build up my confidence.

2022.
My stint as a real estate negotiator went on for another good 8 months. You could probably say I lacked patience, staying on for another year or two could mean more money. But for me, I've always known that I was there only for the experience. And so, I decided to take the safer route and return to the corporate world as a Project Management Consultant in fit-out works.

2023.
Part of me have always wanted to learn coding, espescially during the lockdown period. But as most people do, I always give myself excuses not to - Is it too late to start? Should I really give up a stable job to go for the uncertainties again? Is it worth the opportunity cost? With the way things are moving forward, it's probably safer to make the change now more than ever. So I'm looking forward to the exciting times ahead, wish me luck!
